About Us

ASSOCIATION OF HISPANISTS (OF SERBIA)

In 2007, a group of Hispanic Philology students founded the Association of Hispanists, with the idea of contributing to the expansion of knowledge about the Ibero-American world in Serbia and maintaining a proactive and critical attitude regarding the position of Ibero-American studies in Serbia and the need to learn the Spanish and Portuguese languages.

The Association of Hispanists acts as kind of non-formal education, aiming to bring together undergraduate and graduate students, as well as young activists from social movements, public officials, and members and managers of non-governmental organizations. Its emphasis on an interdisciplinary and informal approach, along with its multi-sectoral reach, fosters a fruitful dialogue among disciplines, individuals, and institutions. These collaborations allow us to develop cultural, educational, and research projects and offer alternative learning and teaching options.
