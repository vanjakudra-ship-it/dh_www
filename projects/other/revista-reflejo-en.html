Revista Reflejo

The official publication of the Association of Hispanists.

Revista Reflejo was founded with the aim of providing students and young researchers with an opportunity to publish their first academic and creative works. The journal covers a wide range of topics related to the Hispanic world, including literature, linguistics, history, and art.

So far, 20 electronic and 5 printed editions have been published. We are particularly proud of our bilingual editions created in collaboration with Hispanic studies departments from the region.

The journal is open for collaboration with all students who wish to contribute to the promotion of Hispanic culture.
