Reflejo Magazine

The official publication of the Association of Hispanists.

Reflejo Magazine was founded within the journalism section as the official electronic publication of the Association of Hispanists, with the aim of making the Association's activities known and sharing experiences of encounters with the richness of Hispanic culture with the Serbian public.

The diverse content represents the spirit of Hispanic culture through multiple aspects: literature, art, music, cinema, sports, politics, rituals and customs of the Hispanic population and ancient civilizations, as well as current events related to the Hispanic world.

The magazine includes literary texts, scientific research, event reports, announcements of current events, translations, interviews and columns.

So far, 20 electronic editions and 5 printed editions have been published (with the support of the Embassies of Spain and Mexico), some of them bilingual (Serbian-Spanish).
