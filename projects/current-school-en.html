Round table

Ten Years of the Summer School of Latin American Studies — achievements and challenges

UK Parobrod, Belgrade
6:00 PM
Wednesday, July 8, 2026

To mark the tenth anniversary of the Summer School of Latin American Studies, a round table entitled "Ten Years of the Summer School of Latin American Studies - achievements and challenges" will be held on Wednesday, July 8 at 6 PM at UK Parobrod.

The discussion will cover the project's achievements to date, its significance, and the future challenges in its development.

Participants

Bojana Kovačević Petrović — Ibero-American Centre, Faculty of Philosophy, University of Novi Sad
Nemanja Džuverović — Faculty of Political Science, University of Belgrade
Dejan Mihailović — Tecnológico de Monterrey, Mexico
Miša Đurković — Institute for European Studies, Serbia
Jelena Spasojević — Association of Hispanists, Serbia

After the round table, a social gathering and pub quiz await us, so we invite you to come and reminisce together about great memories as we celebrate ten years of the Summer School.

See you there!
