VIII Edition: "EVELA 2024"

Main successes

The eighth edition brought together 15 participants from Serbia, Colombia and Ecuador. Nine lecturers from Spain, Italy, Brazil, Chile and Serbia participated.

  • Locations: Belgrade and Novi Sad
  • Date: July 8 - 12, 2024

Organizer: Association of Hispanists of Serbia; Co-organizers: Institute for European Studies, Ibero-American Center of the Faculty of Philosophy, University of Novi Sad.

Supported by: "Parobrod" Cultural Center from Belgrade, Faculty of Media and Communications from Serbia, Professor Andrzej Dembicz Foundation from Poland, Institute for Intercultural and Comparative Research IDEAZ from Austria, NUCLEAS from Brazil and ELASOC project from Spain.

Activities

In addition to lectures, three creative writing workshops were held with Andrea Jeftanović (Chile), and the promotion of two books: "Political systems of Latin American countries" by Rajko Petrović and "Yugoslavia, war and peace in the new Balkan states" by José Ángel Ruiz Jiménez. Bojana Kovačević Petrović promoted the ICA2025 congress. Novi odmetnici presented their music album "Iznad tame (pjesme za Viktora Haru)" dedicated to Víctor Jara, and there was also a pub quiz inspired by Latin America.

Lecturers and topics

Maja T. Izquierdo (SRB): Voices of the Earth: Indigenous Women's Leadership in Environmental and Cultural Defense in Latin America
José Ángel Ruiz Jiménez (ESP): The history of the Balkans from Spain: a spatial fascination
Alexis Dantas (BRA): Brazil and China within BRICS
Aleksandra Perišić (SRB): South-South Dialogue in Migration Literature
Ana Davis i Pablo Sánchez (ESP): Socialist writers and European socialist countries during the Cold War
Gian Luca Gardini (ITA): A contextualized and positive assessment of EU-LAC relations
Rajko Petrović (SRB): The Great Powers and Latin America
Gian Luca Gardini (ITA): Neutrality, South-South Cooperation and the Non-Aligned Movement in Latin America

"Ovogodišnja škola donela je posebnu energiju zahvaljujući direktnom dijalogu između predavača i studenata. Teme ekologije i geopolitičkih odnosa pokazale su se kao ključne tačke spajanja između Balkana i Latinske Amerike."
