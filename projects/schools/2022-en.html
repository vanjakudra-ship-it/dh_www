VI Edition: "EVELA 2022"

Main Achievements

The sixth edition gathered 49 participants from Serbia, Mexico, Argentina, Costa Rica, Brazil, Italy, Ireland, Bulgaria, Romania, Bosnia and Herzegovina.

  • Locations: Belgrade and Novi Sad
  • Date: July 13 - 17, 2022

Organizer: Association of Hispanists of Serbia; Co-organizers: Ibero-American Center of the Faculty of Philosophy, University of Novi Sad, Institute for European Studies.

Supported by: Cultural Center "Parobrod" from Belgrade, Argentine Embassy in Belgrade, Institute for Intercultural and Comparative Research IDEAZ from Vienna, Foundation of Professor Andrzej Dembicz from Warsaw, Center for Latin American Studies of the Faculty of Political Sciences from Belgrade and Center for Social Studies NUCLEAS from Brazil.

Activities

The first day of the Summer School was dedicated to the centenary of the birth of the great Brazilian intellectual Darcy Ribeiro. The colloquium about him was moderated by the president of the Association of Hispanists, Jelena Spasojević, and Professor Dejan Mihailović of the Monterrey Institute of Technology (Mexico). Professor Lia Faria of the State University of Rio de Janeiro participated virtually.

Lecturers and topics

Dejan Mihailović (MX): Rebellion or submission? Latin America in danger of the eternal present
Gido Hercovič (ARG) i Igor Štiks (SRB): Cultures of Hypochondria: Global Life in a Time of Declining Political Expectations
Branko Anđić: Literature and Politics: Alibis in Contemporary Hispanic American Prose - Cuba, Venezuela, Chile
Uroš Ušćebrka: A personal vision of the meeting, that is, the clash of cultures in Mexico
Domingo Lilon (DOM): Latin America through the prism of literature
Bojana Kovačević Petrović (SRB): Mexico's cultural identity from a personal perspective
Andrea Mataljana (ARG): Nelson Rockefeller and the artistic diplomacy of Latin America
Andrej Grubačić (USA): Societies against the State: Politics of Autonomy in Chiapas and Rojava

"Ovogodišnja škola donela je posebnu energiju zahvaljujući direktnom dijalogu između predavača i studenata. Teme ekologije i geopolitičkih odnosa pokazale su se kao ključne tačke spajanja između Balkana i Latinske Amerike."
