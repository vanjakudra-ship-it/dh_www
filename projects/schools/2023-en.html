VII Edition: "EVELA 2023"

Main successes

The seventh edition brought together 30 participants from Serbia, Argentina, Bulgaria, the Czech Republic, Germany, Hungary, Peru, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Romania, Poland, Mexico, Croatia, Spain and Austria. There were four lecturers from Portugal, Serbia, the United States of America and Chile.

  • Location: Online on the Zoom platform
  • Date: July 26 - 29, 2023

The success of the International Summer School of Latin American Studies was made possible by the generous support of several esteemed institutions. We extend our gratitude to the Ibero-American Center at the Faculty of Philosophy in Novi Sad, the Institute for European Studies in Serbia, UK Parobrod from Serbia, the Ilija M. Kolarac Endowment from Serbia, the Professor Andrzej Dembicz Foundation from Poland, the Institute for Intercultural and Comparative Research IDEAZ from Austria, NUCLEAS from Brazil, and CEA-UNC from Argentina.

Activities

As a warm-up for the summer school in 2023, an event of literary conversations with Andrea Jeftanović was held.

Lecturers and topics

Andrés Malamud (PT): Forty years of democracy in Argentina, and what happened to the development? European and Latin-American integrations in times of global turmoil
Gorica Majstorović (USA): Mexican Avant-Garde and Petromodernity
Andrea Jeftanović (CHIL): Making archives speak: documentary writing between words and images in immigrant literature
Bojana Kovačević Petrović (SRB): Literary Encounters with Contemporary Latin American Writers
Andrea Jeftanovic (CHIL): Creative Writing Workshop
Bojana Kovačević Petrović (SRB): Essay Writing Workshop

This year was the last online edition of the summer school, which also had one live lecture. After four online editions, the school returned to live sessions in Serbia, Belgrade and Novi Sad in 2024.
